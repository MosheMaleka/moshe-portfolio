Business Problem

The organization lacked a consolidated view of sales performance. Sales data was spread across multiple Excel sheets and manual reports, resulting in delayed reporting cycles and limited executive visibility.

  • Monthly reports took 5–7 days to compile
  • No centralized KPI tracking
  • Limited insight into regional and product performance
  • Reactive decision-making

Approach

Stakeholder Engagement

Conducted workshops with Sales and Finance teams to define key performance indicators, reporting requirements, and decision-making needs.

ETL Process (Extract, Transform, Load)

Designed and implemented an ETL workflow to extract raw sales data from multiple Excel sources. Data transformation was performed using SQL queries and Python (Pandas) to clean datasets, standardize formats, remove duplicates, and engineer calculated performance metrics.

The transformed data was then loaded into a structured data model within Power BI, enabling automated refresh and consistent KPI reporting.

Analytics & Visualization Layer

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard leveraging DAX measures, dynamic filtering, drill-down functionality, and trend analysis visuals. KPI models were structured to support revenue tracking, regional comparison, and sales representative performance analysis.

Validation & Iteration

Reviewed outputs with stakeholders and refined KPI definitions to ensure alignment with business objectives.

Key Insights

  • One region exceeded targets but had declining margins
  • Underperforming reps lacked diversified product mix
  • 60% of revenue came from one seasonal product category
  • Revenue leakage caused by inconsistent discounting

Technical Architecture

  • Data Sources: Multiple Excel sales reports
  • Transformation Layer: SQL queries + Python (Pandas)
  • Data Modeling: Power BI data model with calculated measures (DAX)
  • Output: Interactive Executive Dashboard with automated refresh

Business Impact

  • Automated ETL pipeline reduced reporting cycle from 5–7 days to under 1 day
  • Enabled proactive performance management
  • Improved regional target tracking visibility
  • Supported strategic revenue growth initiatives
  • Contributed to 12–15% improvement in underperforming regions
